About me

Who I am

I'm a computer science student at Texas A&M, interested in the full stack — from schema design and backend APIs to frontend interfaces. I like systems that are fast, simple, and correct.

Outside of coursework, I build things: a JavaFX POS system backed by PostgreSQL, a self-balancing robot, and a guitar distortion pedal simulated in LTspice and hand-soldered on perf board.

Currently learning more about graph algorithms, distributed systems, and whatever hardware project sounds interesting.
